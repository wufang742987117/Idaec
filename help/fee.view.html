交易费率

1.BTC为分母的比重，交易手续费按固定比例0.2%双向收取BTC，最低手续费0.0001BTC。

   ETP为分母的币种，交易手续费按固定比例0.2%双向收取ETP，最低手续费0.01ETP。

2.主动成交和被动成交费率一致。

3.数字货币提现以所在平台钱包为准，RightBTC平台不收取任何费用。

Transaction rate

1. For BTC trading pairs,the transaction fee are paid both by the buyer and the seller at a fixed ratio of 0.2% BTC. The minimum fee is 0.0001BTC. For ETP trading pairs,the transaction fee are paid both by the buyer and the seller at a fixed ratio of 0.2% ETP. The minimum fee is 0.01ETP.

2. Active and passive transaction rates are the same.

3. The digital currency withdrawal is in accordance with the platform wallet, and the RightBTC platform does not charge any fees.
